#  Google Play Store Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of 10,000+ Play Store apps to understand 
what drives success — ratings, installs and reviews.

## Business Questions Answered
1. Which category has the most apps?
2. What is the distribution of app ratings?
3. Free vs Paid — which gets better ratings?
4. Which categories have most installs?
5. Does app size affect rating?
6. Which category has most reviews?
7. Which category has best average rating?

## Key Findings
- Family category dominates with 1,746 apps
- Most apps rated between 4.0 and 4.5
- Paid apps rate slightly higher than free apps
- Game category leads with 35 billion installs
- Events category has best average rating of 4.48
